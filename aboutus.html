Our Business

Aina Hijab is a local hijab business founded by 4 girls. The idea of starting this business came after discussing with all members. Our main activity is to sell a high-quality hijab with affordable price. Our official supplier is Azzsmallmarket. This company has been in this industry for about 10 years and owned by a man. All of our collection was from this company. In this business, we decided to not employ any employee because this is our first year of business. If our business is more stabilize, we will definitely hire employees.

Our main strategy is doing marketing through online platform. We currently have our official Instagram account and Shopee account. By all these contents, we get to increase our engagement with customers and expand our brand. We also do live stream on Shopee to attract more people. Fortunately, our brand started to grow from our effort through online platform. Last but not least, we really hope that our business will more in future.
